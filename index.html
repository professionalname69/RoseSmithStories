Ripples

Softly dress, wear the souls of angels swelling

And sighing in the valleys of death.

Descend in mist, suspended drops dispelling

The scent of dawn with each hallowed breath.

Drift to the lake, gradient sky submerged, blurred

In the wake of the wind, and step dazed

On waves, unsinkable, distant as fish stirred

From dreams swim beneath faint rays unfazed

As shimmering shades of pink, stretch and distort.

A series of ripples dispersing

As you spin dazzled while light seems to escort,

Drawing you in, slow, no reversing

Shadow and reflection long since departed

In blinding sun devolve, wholehearted

Persephone

I walk barefooted along the river's edge

Staring at murky water and submerged ledge

Gold sandals dangling from my fingertips

Indifferent to the rocks from which my skin rips


Unsettling quiet amplifies each sound.

A distant pole propelled into muddy ground

Stones scuttling down the slope with gravity

Souls' silently wandering eternity


Obscure blurs, detached shadows, drifting, aimless

May reach out to me bewildered and hapless

Clinging to my arm, struggling to convey

Feelings they'd forgotten but they slip away

Warmth

Once upon a time on a cold winter's night

A bird flew fast into the cold moon's light

Her feathers were crumpled and some parts were bear

Her wings couldn't hold her much longer in the air

But she needed to soar along the light once again

Graze the soft snow that she once played in

Hear the song of silence before her last

Feel a warmth before she passed

An old barn she found with a half boarded window

An old nest she found with a light dusting of snow

Made cozy enough with some forgotten hay

She huddled inside with nothing more to say

And wished for the one thing she could not find

Someone to kiss her right before she died

But all was empty all was still

Not a single person around to wish her good will

And so with regret she tucked in her head

And waited for the chill to send her to bed

A child opened the rotting barn yard door

Her eyes looking around at the bare walls and floor
